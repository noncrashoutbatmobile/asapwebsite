Our Achievements

In our inaugural season, A$AP FTC has been honoured with several prestigious awards that reflect our hard work, innovation, and commitment to the principles of FIRST:

2nd Place Inspire Award at the Freedom Prep Qualifier:

This award recognises how we’ve embodied the spirit of FIRST—excelling in robot design, programming, and outreach. It acknowledges our efforts to inspire others with our enthusiasm for robotics and our passion for STEM.

1st Place Innovate Award at the Freedom Prep Qualifier:

We were celebrated for our creative and effective solutions to the game’s engineering challenges, showcasing our team's ingenuity and problem-solving skills in designing our robot, specifically our unique Swerve Drive design.

1st Place Innovate Award at the FPA Phoenix Qualifier:

At the Freedom Prep Academy Phoenix Qualifier, we were honored with this award for showcasing exceptional innovation in our robot's design. Our unique Swerve Drive system stood out as a testament to our creativity and ability to solve complex engineering challenges in groundbreaking ways.

Connect Award at the Utah Championship:

This award recognises our global outreach, where we’ve been mentored by a team in Romania while also mentoring a team in Australia. Our commitment to spreading the word about FIRST and inspiring others to get involved is at the heart of everything we do.

These awards are not just a reflection of our team's success but a reminder of the incredible journey we’ve been on as a rookie team. We’re proud of how far we’ve come and excited for what lies ahead in the world of robotics and STEM.
