Welcome to A$AP FTC

We are a FIRST Tech Challenge team based in South Jordan, Utah. We are a group of high school students who are passionate about STEM and robotics.
We are committed to proving ourselves as a rookie team by developing consistent robotics systems. Based in Salt Lake City, Utah, we aim to revolutionize the world of robotics competitions.
