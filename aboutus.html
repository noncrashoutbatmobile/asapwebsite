About Us

At A$APFTC26482, we are passionate about advancing robotics technology and redefining robot competitions. Our team, established in 2024 by founders Amrit Misra and Joseph Anil, is one of many Utah-based teams.

Our journey began with a shared passion for robotics and a drive for innovation. We wanted to start something unique for the community around us.

At A$AP, we are committed to fostering a culture of inspiration, challenge, and teamwork. Our core values of innovation, integrity, and excellence drive us to push boundaries and set new standards in robotics technology.

The FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) is a global robotics competition that inspires students to innovate and excel in STEM fields. Our team builds robots to compete in these challenges, pushing the boundaries of robotics technology.

We couldn’t achieve our goals without the generosity of our sponsors and donors. Their support helps us push the boundaries of robotics technology and excel in FTC competitions. If you’d like to be part of our journey and support the future of robotics, please consider donating. Every contribution makes a significant difference.
